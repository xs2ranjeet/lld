System Requirements:
1. System should support two online players
2. All rules of international chess will be followed.
Each player will be randomely assigned side, black or white,
Each player will play thrie moves one after the other. The white side plays the first moves.
Player can't cancel or rollback their mvoes.
System should maintain the logs of all moves of both sides.
Each side will start with 8 pawns, 2 rooks, 2 bishop , 2 knight, 1 queen and 1 king.
The game can finish eithe in a checkmate from one side, fordeit or stalemate( a draw) or resignation.
